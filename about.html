About EPR Projects

Your Trusted Construction Partner in East London

EPR Projects Pty-Ltd is a professional construction company serving East London and surrounding areas. We specialize in delivering reliable, durable and high-quality workmanship across residential and commercial projects.

Our services include Roofing, Plumbing, Epoxy Flooring, Waterproofing, Renovations and General Construction. We pride ourselves on attention to detail, timely project delivery and customer satisfaction.

Every project we take on is handled with professionalism, transparency and commitment to excellence.
